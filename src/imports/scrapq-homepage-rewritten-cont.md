## What We Do

### SQ Recycle HUB — Driving Towards Zero Landfills
We stop recyclable waste at the source — households, SMBs, corporates, government organizations, and industries — ensuring it never reaches landfills. As a one-stop sustainability platform, we transform 100+ types of recyclable materials into valuable resources, making recycling truly effortless.

### SQ RoR — Recovery of Recyclables from MRFs
Our innovative Material Recovery Facilities reclaim recyclables from landfills, railway stations, events, and urban hubs. RoR reduces waste at the source, cuts landfill dependency, and fuels the zero-waste mission.

### SQ Circulomony — The True Epitome of a Circular Economy
A people-powered model that ensures fair earnings across the entire ecosystem — from consumers and rag pickers to kabadiwalas, retailers, and wholesalers. By creating shared value, we drive a resilient and inclusive green economy.

### SQ Carbon Tech — Technology to Calculate Your Carbon Footprint
We quantify the carbon footprint of recycled materials, empower consumers with awareness, and help industries move toward minimal emissions — nurturing a greener world, one metric at a time.

### SQ Zero Carbon — Achieve Net Zero Through Recycling
We help industries achieve their net-zero carbon and EPR objectives by delivering sustainability-focused solutions that promote eco-friendly operations at scale.

### SQ RRR — Reduce, Reuse, Recycle Centers
Our EcoStations make e-waste recycling, reduction, and reuse effortless for consumers — fostering environmental stewardship and driving resource efficiency.
